Upcoming Animes

Blue Lock

Audio: Japanese, English
Subtitles: English, Español, Português
Awards: Sports Anime of the Year 2023
Genres: Sports, Psychological, Drama

After Japan’s crushing defeat in the World Cup, the Blue Lock project is launched to find the country’s best striker. 300 high school forwards are pitted against each other in a brutal training facility where only one can emerge victorious. Blue Lock redefines sports anime with intense competition, ego clashes, and an adrenaline-pumping narrative.

Devil May Cry

Audio: Japanese, English
Subtitles: English
Awards: Based on Capcom's Legendary Game
Genres: Action, Supernatural, Demons

Based on the iconic video game series, Devil May Cry follows Dante, a half-demon devil hunter who takes on supernatural jobs to pay off his debts. With stylish action, gothic vibes, and monster-slaying mayhem, the anime expands the world of Devil May Cry with dark flair and signature sword-and-gun combat.

Sword Art Online

Audio: Japanese, English
Subtitles: English, Español, Français
Awards: Best Sci-Fi Anime Series Nominee
Genres: Action, Sci-Fi, Romance, Virtual Reality

When thousands of players get trapped in a VR MMORPG with no escape, death in-game means death in real life. Kirito, a skilled solo player, must survive deadly floors, forge bonds, and uncover the truth behind the game’s creation. Sword Art Online mixes futuristic tech with heartfelt adventure and has become a gateway anime for many fans.

Fire Fire_Force

Audio: Japanese, English
Subtitles: English, Español, Português
Awards: Best Animation Nominee 2020
Genres: Action, Supernatural, Sci-Fi, Shonen

In a world where spontaneous human combustion turns people into destructive fire demons called Infernals, the Fire Force is humanity’s last line of defense. Shinra Kusakabe, a young firefighter with devil's footprints, joins Special Fire Force Company 8 to uncover the truth behind the phenomenon and his mysterious past. With explosive battles and fiery visuals, Fire Force blends intense action with dark secrets and spiritual themes.

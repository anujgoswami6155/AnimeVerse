Action-Oriented Animes

Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba

Audio: Japanese, English
Subtitles: English, Español, Français, Português
Awards: Best Animation, Anime of the Year 2020
Genres: Action, Supernatural, Historical, Drama

Set in Taisho-era Japan, Tanjiro Kamado's peaceful life is shattered when demons slaughter his family and turn his sister Nezuko into one. Vowing revenge and a cure, he joins the Demon Slayer Corps. Known for breathtaking animation, emotional storytelling, and unforgettable characters, Demon Slayer is a modern masterpiece.

Cowboy Bebop

Audio: Japanese, English
Subtitles: English, Español
Awards: Legendary Cult Classic
Genres: Sci-Fi, Action, Drama, Space Western

Set in the year 2071, bounty hunter Spike Spiegel and his crew aboard the spaceship Bebop chase criminals across the solar system. With a jazz-infused soundtrack, mature themes, and stylish visuals, Cowboy Bebop blends Western noir with space opera in a way no anime ever has. It's widely regarded as one of the greatest anime of all time.

Inuyasha

Audio: Japanese, English
Subtitles: English, Español, Português
Awards: Iconic Fantasy Romance
Genres: Adventure, Romance, Supernatural, Historical

Kagome Higurashi, a modern-day schoolgirl, is transported to feudal Japan where she meets the half-demon Inuyasha. Together, they search for the scattered shards of the Shikon Jewel, battling demons and discovering love and destiny. A classic blend of action, romance, and folklore.

Trigun

Audio: Japanese, English
Subtitles: English, Español
Awards: Classic Sci-Fi Western
Genres: Sci-Fi, Action, Comedy, Western

Trigun follows Vash the Stampede, a mysterious gunslinger with a huge bounty and a reputation for destruction—though he’s a pacifist who values life. Set on a desert planet, Trigun is a blend of action, humor, and deep moral dilemmas, wrapped in a stylish space Western aesthetic. The 2023 reboot, *Trigun Stampede*, reintroduces the legend with stunning animation.

Tokyo Revengers

Audio: Japanese, English
Subtitles: English, Español, Português
Awards: Best Drama Anime Nominee 2022
Genres: Action, Drama, Time Travel, Delinquent, Supernatural

Takemichi Hanagaki is a down-on-his-luck adult who discovers he has the power to travel back in time. Determined to save his ex-girlfriend and change his life, he dives into the violent world of Tokyo gangs. As he navigates shifting timelines, rising conflicts, and brotherhoods, Tokyo Revengers delivers emotional punches and high-stakes drama in every arc.

Hell's Paradise

Audio: Japanese, English
Subtitles: English, Español, Português, Français
Awards: 2023 Anime of the Season Nominee
Genres: Action, Dark Fantasy, Supernatural, Samurai

Hell’s Paradise follows Gabimaru the Hollow, a deadly shinobi sentenced to death who’s offered a chance at pardon. His mission? Travel to a mysterious island rumored to hold the elixir of life — but the island is filled with monstrous creatures and eerie secrets. Alongside the executioner Yamada Asaemon Sagiri, Gabimaru must fight for survival in this brutal, beautifully animated dark fantasy.

Re:Zero − Starting Life in Another World

Audio: Japanese, English
Subtitles: English, Español, Português, Deutsch
Awards: Best Fantasy Anime Winner 2016
Genres: Isekai, Fantasy, Psychological, Drama, Romance

Subaru Natsuki is suddenly transported to another world — but this isn’t a typical fantasy adventure. Every time he dies, he’s forced to relive the same moments, trying to change the future and protect those he cares about. With intense emotional stakes, mind-bending loops, and a cast full of depth and mystery, Re:Zero blends psychological drama with high fantasy like no other.

Tougou Anki

Audio: Japanese
Subtitles: English, Español
Awards: N/A
Genres: Mystery, Drama, Supernatural

Tougou Anki is an anime series that delves into the dark and mysterious side of the supernatural. The story follows a group of individuals who become intertwined in a world of complex puzzles, ancient secrets, and a paranormal mystery that tests their minds and hearts. Each episode challenges the characters to face their fears, with intricate plot twists and eerie atmospheres.

Ginga: Nagareboshi Gin

Audio: Japanese
Subtitles: English, Español, Français
Awards: Shogakukan Manga Award (1987)
Genres: Action, Adventure, Drama, Animals

Ginga: Nagareboshi Gin follows the journey of Gin, a young Akita Inu pup, who embarks on a quest to defeat the monstrous bear Akakabuto. Trained by the seasoned hunter Gohei Takeda, Gin joins a group of wild dogs to confront the threat. The series explores themes of bravery, loyalty, and the bond between humans and animals.

Most Popular Animes

Jujutsu Kaisen

Audio: Japanese, English
Subtitles: English, Español, Français, Bahasa Indonesia
Awards: 2023 Anime of the Year
Genres: Action, Supernatural, Dark Fantasy

Jujutsu Kaisen follows Yuji Itadori, a high schooler who becomes host to a powerful curse after swallowing a cursed object. To survive and save others, he joins a secret organization of jujutsu sorcerers battling deadly curses. With stunning animation, intense fights, and emotional depth, it’s a standout in modern shonen anime.

Black Clover

Audio: Japanese, English
Subtitles: English, Español, Português
Awards: Popular New Gen Shonen
Genres: Action, Fantasy, Magic, Adventure

Black Clover tells the story of Asta, a boy born without magic in a world where magic is everything. With sheer determination and a rare anti-magic sword, he strives to become the Wizard King. Alongside his rival Yuno, Asta faces powerful enemies and uncovers dark secrets about their world.

My Hero Academia

Audio: Japanese, English
Subtitles: English, Español, Français
Awards: Multiple Crunchyroll Anime Awards Winner
Genres: Action, Superhero, School, Shonen

In a world where superpowers known as Quirks are common, Izuku Midoriya is born powerless. But after meeting the legendary hero All Might, he inherits the power of One For All and enrolls in U.A. High to become a pro hero. With colorful characters and epic battles, MHA is a modern superhero classic.

Hunter x Hunter

Audio: Japanese, English
Subtitles: English, Español, Português
Awards: Cult Classic
Genres: Adventure, Action, Fantasy, Psychological

Hunter x Hunter follows Gon Freecss, a boy who becomes a Hunter to find his long-lost father. Along the way, he meets friends, faces dangerous foes, and discovers the harsh realities of the Hunter world. With deep storytelling and complex characters, HxH is a fan-favorite with unmatched emotional impact.

Chainsaw Man

Audio: Japanese
Subtitles: English, Español, Bahasa Indonesia
Awards: 2023 Best New Anime
Genres: Action, Horror, Dark Comedy, Supernatural

Denji, a broke devil hunter, merges with his pet devil Pochita and becomes Chainsaw Man — a being with chainsaws for arms. He joins the Public Safety Bureau, fighting devils in exchange for a better life. Wild, violent, and emotionally chaotic, Chainsaw Man breaks every rule of shonen anime.

Dr. Stone

Audio: Japanese, English
Subtitles: English, Español
Awards: 2021 Best Sci-Fi Anime
Genres: Sci-Fi, Adventure, Educational, Comedy

After a mysterious event petrifies humanity, genius teen Senku Ishigami wakes up thousands of years later and begins rebuilding civilization from scratch using science. With exciting inventions, clever humor, and a love for knowledge, Dr. Stone is a thrilling and brainy ride.

Boruto: Naruto Next Generations

Audio: Japanese, English
Subtitles: English, Español, Français
Awards: Continuation of Naruto Saga
Genres: Action, Ninja, Adventure, Drama

Boruto continues the Naruto legacy, focusing on Naruto Uzumaki's son, Boruto, as he navigates the challenges of growing up in the shadow of his famous father. With new threats, futuristic tech, and fresh characters, Boruto explores legacy, change, and growth in the ninja world.

Mob Psycho 100

Audio: Japanese, English
Subtitles: English, Español, Bahasa Indonesia
Awards: Best Animation Winner
Genres: Action, Comedy, Supernatural, Psychological

Shigeo "Mob" Kageyama is a quiet boy with immense psychic powers. As he tries to live a normal life, he faces emotional turmoil and dangerous enemies. With brilliant animation, heartfelt themes, and mind-blowing battles, Mob Psycho 100 is a masterpiece of balance.

Spy x Family

Audio: Japanese, English
Subtitles: English, Español, Français
Awards: Best Comedy Anime 2022
Genres: Action, Comedy, Slice of Life, Spy

Spy x Family follows Twilight, a top spy who must create a fake family to complete a mission. What he doesn’t know? His "wife" is an assassin and his adopted daughter is a telepath. This wholesome yet action-packed comedy has taken the anime world by storm with its unique charm.

Blue Lock

Audio: Japanese
Subtitles: English, Español, Bahasa Indonesia
Awards: Best Sports Anime 2023
Genres: Sports, Psychological, Action

After Japan’s national soccer team fails at the World Cup, a radical program called Blue Lock is created to forge the world’s best striker. Ego, rivalry, and self-discovery collide in this intense soccer survival anime. Blue Lock redefines sports anime with its raw energy and mind games.
